People-Powered Research

The Zooniverse provides opportunities for people around the world to contribute to real discoveries in fields ranging from astronomy to zoology. Welcome to the largest online platform for collaborative volunteer research.

Get Involved Now!
Contribute to new research

The Zooniverse lets everyone take part in real, cutting-edge research in many fields across the sciences, humanities, and more. There’s no previous experience required; just pick a project and get started right away.

Explore incredible worlds

Astronomical marvels, exotic wildlife in their natural habitats, original historical documents: just a few of the fascinating things you’ll get to experience in the Zooniverse. In many cases, you’ll literally be seeing things no one has seen before.

Collaborate with researchers

Professional researchers and volunteers share our discussion boards, using them to explore and analyse project data. Much of the most exciting research produced by the Zooniverse originates from these partnerships.

Discover, teach, and learn

Our platform offers many opportunities for education, from using projects in classrooms to sharing information between volunteers. You can even use the Zooniverse Project Builder to create your very own project!
